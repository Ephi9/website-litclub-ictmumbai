Who We Are

The Literary Club is more than just a club; it's a sanctuary for bibliophiles and aspiring wordsmiths at ICT Mumbai. We host a variety of events, from intense debates and The Spirit Days to relaxed reading sessions and author talks. Our mission is to foster a culture of literary appreciation and critical thinking within the campus.

Engage

Participate in stimulating discussions, workshops, and competitions that challenge your perspective.

Create

Unleash your creativity through creative writing sessions, open mic nights, and our annual magazine publication.

Connect

Meet like-minded individuals, network with authors, and build lasting friendships based on a shared love for literature.
