Who We Are

The Literary Club is more than just a club; it's a sanctuary for bibliophiles and aspiring wordsmiths at ICT Mumbai. We host a variety of events, from intense debates and poetry slams to relaxed reading sessions and author talks.

Engage

Participate in stimulating discussions, workshops, and competitions that challenge your perspective.

Create

Connect

Meet like-minded individuals, network with authors, and build lasting friendships.
